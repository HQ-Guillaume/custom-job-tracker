# Code Of Conduct

This project welcomes constructive, respectful collaboration.

## Expected Behavior

- Be clear, kind, and specific in discussions and reviews.
- Respect different levels of experience with PowerShell, crawling, and job-search
  workflows.
- Keep feedback focused on the work, evidence, and user impact.
- Avoid sharing private job-search data, credentials, cookies, or personal
  application history.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, or intimidation.
- Publishing another person's private information.
- Sharing credentials, cookies, generated trackers, personal notes, or private
  job-search data.
- Repeated low-effort disruption after maintainers have asked for a change in
  behavior.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments, issues, pull requests, or other
contributions that do not follow this code. Serious or repeated issues may lead
to blocking from the project.
